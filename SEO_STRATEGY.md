# FloorStay Strategic Workflow: SEO & Price Dominance

## The "Winner's Circle" Strategy
The goal is not just to compare prices, but to ensure FloorStay is the **authority** for your properties. We leverage the SEO power of Airbnb/VRBO to drive traffic to your direct site.

## 1. SEO Interception (The "Leech" Effect)
- **Keyword Targeting**: We automatically inject high-intent keywords into your storefront:
  - `[Property Name] + "Airbnb Price"`
  - `[Property Name] + "Official Booking Site"`
  - `[Property Name] + "VRBO Discount"`
- **Trust Anchors**: Displaying "Airbnb Verified Property" badges on your direct site leverages their brand trust while stealing the conversion.

## 2. Dynamic Price Dominance
- **Service Fee Arbitrage**: OTAs add 12-18% on top of your base price. FloorStay displays this delta as a "Platform Tax," positioning your direct price as the "True Floor Price."
- **Real-Time Guardrails**: If an OTA runs a promotion, our backend logic detects the shift and ensures your Direct Price remains at least 5% lower or adds a high-value perk (e.g., "Free Parking") to preserve the "Winning" status.

## 3. The "Direct-Only" Metadata
- We generate schema markup that tells Google your FloorStay site is the **canonical owner** of the property listing, increasing your ranking over aggregate OTA results for direct name searches.

## 4. AI Conversion Layer
- Your Tenant AI isn't just helpful; it's a closer. If a guest mentions they saw it on Airbnb, the AI is trained to offer an instant "Direct Booking Incentive" (e.g., dynamic coupon code) to prevent them from returning to the OTA.
